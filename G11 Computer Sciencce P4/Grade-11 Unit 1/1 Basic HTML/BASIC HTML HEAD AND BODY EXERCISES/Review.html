VALORANT

  1. Graphics

    2. Valorant has 3 types of settings for graphics, High, Medium, and Low.
    The high setting makes all of the elements in the game have more reflection
    and shine to it whereas the meduim setting has less shine but more texture.
    The low setting has the least amount of everything where there are no textures
    and shine/reflection.

  2. Gameplay

    3. The way to play the game is there are 19 characters that are choosable in the
    loading screen. You and 4 of your teammates choose 5 out of the 19 characters
    and fight against 5 other people online. The game is out of 13 rounds in where
    each round you either try to kill everyone else or plant a bomb and let it blow up.
    the game ends first to 13 and there is douce where if both teams are at 12 points,
    2 rounds wins.

  3. Storyline

    4. The game is a 5v5 tactical shooter where over 19 million peoplep play monthly
    There are many cheaters that have aim bot, wall hacks and many other types of

  4. Sound

    5. In Valorant sound is very important, it helps you locate where enemies are
    and can help with telling what is happening outside of your POV. Skins for
