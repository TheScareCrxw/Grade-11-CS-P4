    Rules

  1. Massage Mayhem begins with all 3 parts of the back (top, mid, and lower) visible on the screen.
  2. Every 10 seconds the program randomly chooses which part of the back needs a massage. Based on the random generation, massage the chosen part of the back.
  3. Each click on the correct part of the back is worth $1. Each click on the wrong part of the back is worth nothing.
  4. Every 5 Mintues there is a chance you can get robbed. You will lose 50% of your money.

    Upgrades

  1. Extra Muscular Fingers. Initial upgrade cost is $100. Each additional muscle upgrade costs an extr 10% of the previous cost. Each muscular finger upgrade gives an extra $5 per click. After 5 upgrades, you can injur yourself, losing 100$. This caps at 10 fingers.
  2. Incredibly Attractive Assistant. Initial upgrade cost is $400. Each additional incredibly attractive assistant costs an extra 15% of the previous cost. Produces 1$ per second.
  3. Ambidextrous. This upgrade can only be upgraded once and it doubles your money earned each click after adding Extra Muscular Fingers value.
  4. Franchise. Initial upgrade cost is $5000. Each additional franchise costs an extra 10% of the previous cost. Each franchise gives an extra $200 per second. Each additional franchise offers only 95% of the amount given by the previous franchise.
  5. Initial Public Offering: This only becomes available after the user has played for 10 minutes, and it can be accepted only once. If enabled, the user immediately gets $50,000.
  6. Relocate: This costs $10000 each time it is done. It reduces the crime rate by 20% with each relocation. It is cumulative.
  7. Hire Security: This costs $5 per second. It reduces the amount of money robbed, if it happens, by 50%. Each additional security still costs $5 per second. Effects are cumulative meaning, that they also reduce the amount lost by 50%.
